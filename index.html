About the Firm

Where Financial Integrity
Meets Expert Counsel

NS Panicker Chartered Accountants is a distinguished CA practice headquartered in Chinnakada, Kollam—built on decades of trust, ethical practice, and deep financial expertise.

We serve individuals, SMEs, corporate houses, and NRIs with a full spectrum of accounting, taxation, audit, and advisory services. Our client-centric philosophy ensures every engagement is handled with absolute precision, confidentiality, and personalised attention.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Financial Services

From tax planning to audit assurance, we cover every aspect of your financial and compliance needs with elite-level professionalism.

Tax Consultation

Strategic tax planning and advisory for individuals, companies, and HUFs to optimise liability and ensure complete compliance.

GST Filing & Compliance

End-to-end GST registration, return filing, reconciliation, and advisory to keep your business fully compliant under GST law.

Auditing & Assurance

Statutory, internal, and tax audit services delivered with meticulous diligence—ensuring accuracy, accountability, and regulatory adherence.

Company Registration

Seamless incorporation of Private Limited, LLP, OPC, and partnership firms with end-to-end MCA compliance and secretarial support.

Financial Advisory

Sound financial guidance on investment planning, project financing, business valuation, and wealth structuring for long-term growth.

Income Tax Filing

Accurate and timely ITR filing for salaried individuals, business owners, NRIs, and corporates with maximum exemption optimisation.

Compliance Services

ROC filings, TDS compliance, FEMA advisory, and regulatory submissions managed proactively to eliminate compliance risk.

Business Accounting

Comprehensive bookkeeping, management accounts, payroll processing, and MIS reporting tailored to your business needs.
